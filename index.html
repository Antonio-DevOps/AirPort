    Excursions

    Do you want to open the veil of secrets of the airport? Then we invite you to the backstage of the largest air harbor in Eastern Ukraine.

    Our guided tours, which are available to everyone, will take you to the new Terminal A, check-in desks and arrivals and departures with breathtaking panoramic views of the runway.

    Our experienced guides will devote you to all the details of preparing aircraft for flights and will show you the inside of the air harbor, namely: hangars, where small aircraft are presented, a fire station and an apron from which you can observe the takeoffs and landings of aircraft. And of course, as part of the tour, you will see the "holy of holies" of all airports - the central control room.

    You will also plunge into the history of Ukrainian aviation and the Kharkiv airport and visit its first terminal, which is an architectural monument and currently serves as a VIP terminal.
